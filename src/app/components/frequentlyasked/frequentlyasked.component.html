Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to order from Niqasay?

Ordering from Niqasay is actually pretty easy! All you will need to do is to follow three simple steps:
1. Browse for the product you are looking for.
2. Fill the order form on the top right of each page. An order number will can be seen at Top Navigation -> Orders after you logIn
3. Our friendly customer support team will contact you for order confirmation
4. Post confirmations you will get the delivery of your desired product in a few days
5. If any case call us on our contact Numbers.
